What We Detect

Every trick fake reviewers use,
exposed.

Our rule engine checks for over 8 distinct signals associated with fake, incentivized, or bot-generated reviews.

🗣️
Language Pattern Analysis
Detects excessive superlatives, generic phrasing, and template-like sentence structures common in paid reviews.
😐
Sentiment Authenticity
Checks if the emotional tone is natural or artificially inflated with false enthusiasm and forced positivity.
🔁
Repetition Detection
Identifies overused words and phrases that indicate bot-generated or copy-pasted review content.
📋
Specificity Scoring
Real reviews mention prices, dates, product variants, and personal comparisons. We check if any of these exist.
⭐
Rating vs Content Match
5-star reviews with zero criticism are suspicious. We cross-verify the star rating against the actual written content.
👤
Reviewer Profile Signals
Generic usernames like "HappyBuyer2024" correlate with fake account networks — we flag these patterns.
